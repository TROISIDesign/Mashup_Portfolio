    One of my most grandiose and ambitous projects has been underway for years, and will be an ongoing labor of love as it evolves and finds its space in the market.


    The idea is to have ONE APP, for every single thing you want or need or didn't even know you need on the web. Inspired by the stock trading platform "Think Or Swim", it is a Desktop, Web, and Mobile Console that has moveable modules for every type of inforation IN & OUT, all in one location. It can be easily styled by the user, and heavily customized.


    All of your email addresses flow into one email repository and are sorted and managed with the goal of inbox zero. Every social media feed is reverse-forked, while maintaining enough separation and styles to mimic the source UI/UX. Youtube videos, Discord server lists, Facebook and Instagram feeds, are all contained in their unique, seperate "panels", which are moveable, sizeable, styleable and customizeable to what works for you. It also contains a Chromium browser, with the ability to add content from any website to a new Panel with a single click.
