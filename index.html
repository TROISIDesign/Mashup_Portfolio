About Me

I am a passionate developer who is always looking for dynamic new ideas and challenges.
My career has spanned a very wide swath outside the realm of software development. I have been, and still am... a Product Manager, a Program Manager, an Analog Electronics Hardware Designer, an Audio Mastering Engineer, a Video Editor, a Photographer, a Yoga Instructor, a Founder, a Co-Founder, and a President and CEO. However, nothing is as freeing and gratifying as being able to compile code based on an idea and have something created before my eyes. For this reason, I consider myself to be a developer First.
