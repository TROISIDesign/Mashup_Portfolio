MONOPOLY


This is a new take on the classic board game, Monopoly. Designed to be played over several hours, days, weeks, or even months, it allows you to connect socially and play the game with friends. Once two or more friends begin the game, they select their token, and the board is drawn and money distributed. The server renders all positions after each move, and a status bar displays both purchases/ownership of properties as well as a log of every move and transaction.
